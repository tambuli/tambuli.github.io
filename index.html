Bridging the last mile

Our vision is to provide last-mile connectivity to the Philippines and beyond
with Community Cellular Networks.

small-scale, community-centric,
bottom-up cellular networks

Community Cellular Networks are smaller, “no frills” base stations,
which drastically reduce the costs required for equipment, setup, power and maintenance.

Tambuli has developed and will pilot the Village Base Station (VBTS) -- a full-size GSM (2.5G)
hardware and software cellular solution optimized for deployment in small-scale, rural, off-grid communities.

About Tambuli

Tambuli is a Filipino word that roughly translates to English as “foghorn” or “trumpet”,
which we believe serves as a compelling metaphor for the potential for the
Community Cellular Network to create new possibilities for rural communities.

Tambuli is comprised of seven graduates of the University of the Philippines - Diliman
and has deep expertise in wireless communications, RF circuit design, software development,
and embedded systems. The team also has integration and deployment experience from
working with several university research projects dating back to 2011.
